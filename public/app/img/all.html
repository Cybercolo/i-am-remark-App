#IamRemarkable Week Talks & Panels, 12-19 November 2020
#IamRemarkable Week is a digital experience celebrating the power of diversity, inclusion and allyship. Get inspired through virtual talks and panels with influential thinkers, creators and business leaders from all around the world. Discover more on the #IamRemarkable week site - g.co/iar-week-2020. 

#IamRemarkable is a Google initiative that strives to empower everyone, particularly women and underrepresented groups, to celebrate their achievements in the workplace and beyond. Up to November 2020, #IamRemarkable has reached 150,000 participants across 140+ countries, with the help of 7000+ facilitators.

To join #IamRemarkable go to g.co/IamRemarkable
